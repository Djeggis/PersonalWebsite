ORIGIN OF "DJEGGIS"

The name "Djeggis" was actually coined by one of my other friends. In the game "Rainbow Six Siege" there is a character named "Jäger"; my friend liked to give funny nicknames to the characters in the game, and this character was coined as "Djeggis". At the time I was playing alot of Siege, specifically Jäger, and I took a liking to the name, especially since I wanted to get a new net tag. Thus, I took on the alias "Djeggis".
